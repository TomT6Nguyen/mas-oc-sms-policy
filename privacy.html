MAS-OC Alerts — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-28

MAS-OC Alerts ("we") is an automated application that sends one-way, operational and system alert text messages (SMS) to the authorized owner-operator who has opted in to receive them. This is an application-to-person (A2P) notification service — the application is the sender, the owner-operator is the recipient — not an interactive or person-to-person (P2P) messaging service.

How you consent to SMS messages

Consent is collected inside the application. The owner-operator enters and verifies their own mobile number in the authenticated, password-protected configuration and explicitly enables the "Operational SMS Alerts" channel — an affirmative, express opt-in to receive these automated alerts. There is no public sign-up form because recipients are limited to the business's own authorized owner-operator; there are no third-party recipients. The full consent flow is shown at SMS Opt-In & Consent. Consent can be withdrawn at any time by replying STOP, or by disabling the alert channel in the same configuration.

Information we collect

A mobile phone number, provided by the owner-operator solely to deliver these alert messages.

How we use it

The mobile number is used only to send the owner-operator the operational/system alerts they requested. We do not use it for marketing.

No sharing or selling of mobile information

We do not share or sell mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in information, or any personal information to third parties or lead generators — for any purpose. Mobile opt-in data is never shared with anyone.

Message frequency and rates

Message frequency varies based on system activity; you may receive recurring messages. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP at any time to opt out of messages. Reply HELP for help.
